I began with a open source file for a spur gear and then made changes to the overall size, number of teeth, and height/extrusion. I measured the "pin" using a caliper and created a center hole = 5.85mm, 2mm larger than the rod to ensure the base would fit through the opening.

I designed the "shade" piecies in AI—omg So easy LOL.I put the file on the Mill computer and chose settings of 5/speed, 100
for both frequency/power.I tested before printing on a scrap.
